Dr. Aklilu Lemma

The Scientist Who Revolutionized Disease Control in Africa

Timeline:

  • 1934 - Born in Jijiga, Ethiopia
  • 1964 - Discovers *endod* plant's ability to kill snails carrying schistosomiasis
  • 1970s - Conducts in-depth research on *endod* as a natural pesticide
  • 1983 - Receives the Right Livelihood Award
  • 1997 - Passes away, leaving a lasting legacy in global health
